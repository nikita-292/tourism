About Bharat Bhavan

Bharat Bhavan, located in Bhopal, is a multi-arts complex and cultural hub dedicated to showcasing and promoting various forms of arts and culture. Established in 1982, Bharat Bhavan serves as a platform for artists, performers, and enthusiasts to celebrate the rich diversity of Indian art and heritage.

The complex houses various facilities, including an art gallery, a performing arts theater, a museum, and a library. Bharat Bhavan is designed to foster creativity and provide a space for artists and art lovers to connect and appreciate the beauty of different art forms.

History of Bharat Bhavan

Bharat Bhavan was conceived and established by the renowned Hindi poet and writer, Sumitranandan Pant. The idea was to create a space that promotes and preserves the diverse cultural heritage of India. The construction of Bharat Bhavan was spearheaded by the visionary artist, Roopankar, and it was inaugurated in 1982.

Over the years, Bharat Bhavan has played a pivotal role in promoting arts and culture, hosting various events, exhibitions, and performances that celebrate the richness of Indian creativity.
