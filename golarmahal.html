About Golar Mahal

Golar Mahal, situated in the heart of Bhopal, is a historical palace known for its intricate architecture and cultural significance. Built during the reign of the Nawabs of Bhopal, Golar Mahal stands as a testament to the city's royal heritage.

The palace showcases a blend of Indo-Islamic and Mughal architectural styles, featuring ornate carvings, graceful arches, and beautiful domes. Golar Mahal has been a center for cultural and social gatherings, adding to its historical importance.

History of Golar Mahal

Golar Mahal was commissioned by the ruling Nawabs of Bhopal in the 19th century. It served as a residence for the nobility and played a crucial role in hosting royal events and celebrations. Over the years, Golar Mahal has retained its charm and remains a key landmark in Bhopal.
